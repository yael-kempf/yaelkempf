But Run Rabbit Run

As those wild rabbits living in this parking lot, we are playing as well in a democratic society. If life is a video game, why can’t they take part with us? What interests me is to go back to the basis of our childhood, when we didn’t distinguish between animals and humans. When your dog was your brother. Where do our rules come from? What defines us as human beings? What is the nature of these «wild» rabbits?
